Cyber Security

What is Cyber Security?

Cyber Security is the practice of protecting computers, networks, and data from unauthorized access or attacks. It helps to keep personal, business, and government data safe.

Why is it Important?

Without Cyber Security, hackers can steal information, cause financial loss, or damage systems. Everyone using the internet is at risk without proper protection.

Types of Cyber Threats

  • Phishing: Fake emails or messages that steal personal info
  • Malware: Harmful software like viruses and spyware
  • Ransomware: Locks your files and demands payment
  • Hacking: Unauthorized access to data or systems

Simple Safety Tips

  1. Use strong passwords
  2. Update your software regularly
  3. Use antivirus software
  4. Don't click on unknown links or downloads
  5. Be careful on public Wi-Fi
